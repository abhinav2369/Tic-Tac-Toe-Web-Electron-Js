Tic Tac Toe

 Welcome to Tic Tac Toe! Enjoy this classic game of strategy and fun. Let's play and see who wins!"

RULES

  • Player 1 is 'X' and Player 2 is 'O
  • Players take turns marking empty cells with their symbol.
  • The first to align three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins.
  • If all cells are filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
  • Block your opponent's moves while planning your own winning sequences.
  • Follow the rules and enjoy friendly competition!

ABOUT

Tic Tac Toe is an open-source, classic game where two players compete to form a row, column, or diagonal of their symbol.

Enjoy endless entertainment with friends and family.

Simple rules make it accessible to players of all ages.

Enhance your strategic thinking and have fun challenging your opponents.

Available for free, bringing timeless fun to your fingertips.
